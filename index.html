WHO WE ARE

CREATING VISUAL MASTERPIECES

Monkey Monk Studio is a creative motion graphics and 3D visualization studio specializing in high-impact anamorphic visuals for brands, retail spaces, and large-format LED displays.

We blend the playful creativity of a Monkey Mind with the focused clarity of a Monk Mind to create bold, immersive, and attention-grabbing experiences that go beyond screens and turn displays into moments people stop and watch.

Founded by Murali Krishnan, a VFX artist with 8+ years of experience working at world-leading VFX studios on top Hollywood film productions, we bring cinematic, high-end VFX craftsmanship into futuristic advertising and anamorphic 3D visuals.

Our Mission

To fuse cinematic VFX craftsmanship with next-generation motion graphics and anamorphic 3D, creating visuals that pull people into the experience and transform digital displays into powerful storytelling platforms.

Our Vision

To become India's leading creative studio and one of the world's top anamorphic advertising companies, setting global benchmarks in immersive 3D, motion graphics, and futuristic digital experiences.
